## A New Model for Software

We are the **Lean C.R.O.W.D.** — a non-profit professional business merging a regenerative software consultancy with a socio-technical think-tank. We operate as a **Design Science Laboratory**, where philosophy meets mechanical application.

### Our Mission

To make the existing, manipulative software models obsolete by building a new model focused on **net positive** contributions to both people and the planet.

Our work is guided by five core values:

- **Curious** — Comprehensive thinking to understand whole systems
- **Regenerative** — Moving beyond sustainability to active restoration
- **Optimize** — Eliminating waste through green algorithms and lean coding
- **Whole** — Systems thinking applied to software as a living ecology
- **DORITH** — Do the Right Thing, prioritizing integrity over short-term gain

### Three Pillars

**The Think-Tank** provides the "What" and "Why" — research into socio-technical transitions, digital sustainability, and regenerative computing.

**The Consultancy** provides the "How" — applying Lean, DevOps, and Continuous Delivery to build software that serves humanity without exploitation.

**The Community of Practice** unites employees, client-members, and contributors in democratic governance, shared costs, and common purpose.
