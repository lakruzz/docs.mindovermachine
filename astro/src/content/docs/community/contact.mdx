## FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: Is the Lean C.R.O.W.D. accepting new members/clients?**

A: Yes! We're actively growing and welcoming members aligned with our regenerative mission. Reach out to explore fit.

**Q: Are you hiring for my specific role/skill?**

A: Very possibly. Our needs evolve. Contact us with your background, and we'll discuss opportunities.

**Q: Do I need to be based in Denmark?**

A: No. We're remote-first. Many team members work from other countries.

**Q: Do you work with offshore teams or outsourced labor?**

A: We practice what we preach. We don't outsource work to low-wage regions. Our team members are fairly compensated, benefited, and valued.

### Membership & Partnership

**Q: How much does it cost to work with you?**

A: Varies by engagement type and scope. We use capability-based pricing, not hourly billing. Contact us for estimates.

**Q: Do you work with "traditional" software companies?**

A: If they're committed to regenerative principles, absolutely. We've worked with startups, non-profits, agencies, and enterprises. Fit matters more than size.

**Q: How long are engagements typically?**

A: 12–24 months minimum. We work in partnership, not project-by-project. Longer-term engagements are better for real transformation.

**Q: Can I try you out for a smaller project first?**

A: We can discuss. Typically we recommend starting with a Strategic Assessment (1–2 weeks) to explore fit before full engagement.

### Employment & Fellowship

**Q: What's the visa situation for non-EU employees?**

A: We're exploring options. Denmark has frameworks for skilled workers. Contact us to discuss your situation.

**Q: Do you have remote work options?**

A: Yes, remote-first for most roles. Some in-person time may be expected for specific roles (1–2x/year minimum).

**Q: What's the salary range for different levels?**

A: See our [compensation page](/careers/compensation/) for published ranges. All salaries are transparent and non-negotiable (to ensure equity).

**Q: How much vacation do I get?**

A: Minimum 25 days (Danish standard). Many team members take 30+. We encourage time off.

**Q: Can I apply to the fellowship if I'm not in the EU?**

A: We're exploring options for international fellows. Contact us to discuss.

### Open Source & Contributing

**Q: Can I contribute to open-source projects even if I'm not a Lean C.R.O.W.D. employee?**

A: Of course! We encourage everyone to contribute. We support our team's OS work and champion the community.

**Q: Which open-source projects do you support?**

A: See our [projects directory](/commons/projects/). We welcome suggestions for new projects.

**Q: Can my organization get funding for an open-source project?**

A: Possibly. We allocate 50% of profit to vetted OS projects. Submit to nomination process on our [projects page](/commons/projects/).
