## Contributing to the Lean C.R.O.W.D

You don't need to be an employee or client to support our mission. There are many ways to contribute:

### Open Source Contributions

**Help advance the projects we fund.**

- Contribute code to Linux, Debian, GNOME, or other projects we support
- Improve documentation and accessibility
- Help with community moderation and support
- Share your expertise as a mentor

**We'll:**

- List you in our community contributors
- Help connect you with project maintainers
- Consider funding your work if aligned with our priorities

### Thought Partnership

**Advance regenerative thinking through collaboration.**

- Write case studies or research on digital sustainability
- Participate in our think-tank discussions
- Contribute to field guides and knowledge sharing
- Speak at DevOpsDays or events about regenerative principles

**We'll:**

- Publish your work
- Provide platforms and distribution
- Collaborate on research projects
- Compensate significant contributions

### Fund-Contributor Support

**Provide financial resources to sustain our work.**

- **Impact investing** — Fund our organization's growth
- **Project sponsorship** — Support specific open-source projects we fund
- **Research grants** — Support our think-tank work
- **Equipment or resources** — Donate tools or services

**We'll:**

- Provide transparency on where funds go
- Annual reporting on impact
- Recognition (unless anonymity preferred)
- Voice in governance (for significant contributions)

### Speaking & Advocacy

**Help spread regenerative principles in the software industry.**

- Speak at conferences about our work
- Share your experience learning regenerative principles
- Advocate for ethical software development
- Mentor others in your organization

**We'll:**

- Provide talking points and resources
- Feature your story on our platform
- Connect you with speaking opportunities
- Support your growth as a thought leader

### Community Building

**Help grow the Community of Practice.**

- Organize local DevOpsDays or community events
- Host workshops on regenerative principles
- Build study groups or learning communities
- Welcome and onboard new members

**We'll:**

- Provide materials and support
- Help with logistics
- Promote your events
- Connect you with regional community

### Advisory & Expertise

**Serve on advisory boards or provide strategic guidance.**

- Join our Professional Board (2-year commitment)
- Provide domain expertise on specific initiatives
- Help us navigate challenges (governance, growth, strategy)
- Mentor leadership

**We'll:**

- Provide clear terms and expectations
- Compensate appropriately
- Support your learning about our work
- Integrate you into community

### Employment

**Work with us as an employee or contractor.**

- **Full-time roles** — Across capability levels
- **Contract work** — Project-based or specialized tasks
- **Fellowship** — Learn through apprenticeship

[Learn about careers](/careers/capability-matrix/) or [apply to fellowship](/fellowship/apply/).

## Sharing Our Work

**Want to amplify our mission?**

- Blog posts or articles about what we're doing
- Social media sharing of our content
- Recommending us to organizations seeking regenerative approaches
- Connecting us with potential members or fund-contributors

**No formal commitment needed** — Just share if our work resonates with you.

### Recognition

**Contributors are recognized through:**

- Listed on our website (with link to your work if applicable)
- Featured in our annual impact reports
- Highlighted in research publications
- Invited to community celebrations and events
