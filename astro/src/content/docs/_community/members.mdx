## Join the Community of Practice

As a **client-member**, you're not a traditional vendor-customer relationship. You're a partner in our Community of Practice, with democratic voice in our strategic direction.

### What Does Membership Mean?

**Access:**

- Our specialized team of regenerative engineers (Levels 1–6)
- Participatory design workshops with your users
- Strategic advisory on regenerative principles
- Access to our think-tank research and field guides
- Connection to the broader community of practice

**Participation:**

- Vote in quarterly General Assembly meetings
- Voice in which open-source projects we fund
- Influence over our strategic direction and methodology evolution
- Participation in research and case studies

**Commitment:**

- 12–24 month engagements (not one-off projects)
- Fair share of joint effort costs
- Active engagement (not passive vendor relationship)
- Alignment with regenerative principles

### Types of Engagements

**Software Angel Startups**

- Early-stage companies (seed to Series A)
- Core work is building software
- Need regenerative engineering foundation
- Benefit from our talent network and methodology
- Typical investment: 8–16 weeks initial build, then ongoing optimization

**Mission-Driven Non-Profits**

- Established non-profit organizations
- Need technology to fully embody their mission
- Want infrastructure that doesn't exploit users or staff
- Seeking Tech Strategy Advisory on long-term digital roadmap
- Typical engagement: Strategic review (4 weeks) → Implementation (6–12 months)

**"Downward Slope" Resilience Projects**

- Organizations concerned with organizational resilience
- Need ICT that can operate within planetary boundaries
- Seeking Advisory on regenerative principles
- Want to understand true cost of their digital systems
- Typical engagement: Research + Advisory (ongoing)

**"Ethical Tech" Collectives**

- Tech-forward cooperatives and social enterprises
- Want to build product with participatory design
- Seeking technology partners who share values
- Need help navigating green algorithms and sustainable operations
- Typical engagement: Full-cycle product development + ongoing optimization

### The Member Agreement

The **Regenerative Member Agreement** establishes mutual commitments:

**From us:**

- Apply regenerative SDLC rigorously to your projects
- Involve users participatorily in all design
- Conduct our work with mechanical application of lean principles
- Be transparent about costs, timelines, and decisions
- Measure impact beyond velocity metrics

**From you:**

- Commit to 12–24 month engagement (not project-by-project)
- Participate in governance and community events
- Make decisions in good faith aligned with regenerative mission
- Facilitate user participation in design
- Be transparent with us about constraints and goals

### Membership Process

**1. Discovery Call** (30 minutes)

- Your goals, challenges, constraints
- Our approach and how we differ from typical agencies
- Preliminary assessment of fit

**2. Strategic Assessment Engagement** (1–2 weeks)

- Deep dive into your current state
- Interviews with key stakeholders
- Assessment of regenerative opportunity
- Recommendation for direction

**3. Member Agreement Negotiation**

- Clarify mutual commitments
- Establish governance and communication
- Define success metrics aligned with regenerative impact

**4. Engagement Begins**

- Assigned team and mentors
- Integrated into Community of Practice
- Regular General Assembly participation

### Costs & Pricing

**Not** a traditional "hourly billing" model.

**Instead:**

- **Capability-based pricing** — Based on which service levels are engaged
- **Transparent costs** — You know what you're paying for
- **Value-based** — We're compensated based on impact, not just time
- **Shared success** — When you succeed, we succeed

_Specific pricing varies by engagement type. Contact us for details._
